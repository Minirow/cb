About the Game

SCP - Containment Breach Reborn is a fan-made remake of the popular horror game SCP - Containment Breach. The game features updated graphics, improved AI, and new levels and content.

Gameplay

The gameplay of SCP - Containment Breach Reborn is similar to the original game. The player must navigate a facility filled with dangerous SCPs while avoiding security and escaping the facility.

Features

  • Improved graphics
  • New levels and content
  • Updated AI
  • Free to play
