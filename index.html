SCP - Containment Breach Reborn

SCP - Containment Breach Reborn is a remake of the popular indie horror game SCP - Containment Breach.

About the game

The game is set in a secret research facility containing anomalous objects and creatures that pose a threat to humanity. You play as a test subject who must escape the facility while avoiding deadly SCPs and solving puzzles.
